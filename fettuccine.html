Creamy Mushroom Fettuccine Recipe

How to Make ​Mushroom ​Fettuccine

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Mushroom Fettuccine Ingredients

  • Pasta: Of course, you’ll need fettuccine pasta.
  • Butter: This sauce starts with two sticks of butter.
  • Mushrooms: We need them too.
  • Cream:The rich sauce calls for almost a cup of heavy cream.
  • Seasonings:The fettuccine is simply seasoned with salt, pepper, and garlic salt

How to Make Homemade ​​Mushroom Fettuccine

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.

Bon appetit!
